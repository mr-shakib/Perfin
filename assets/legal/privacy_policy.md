# Privacy Policy for PerFin

Effective date: April 7, 2026

PerFin helps you manage personal finances. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## 1. Data We Collect

- Account data: name, email, authentication identifiers.
- Finance data you provide: transactions, budgets, goals, notes, and settings.
- App preferences: theme, onboarding settings, notification preferences.
- Technical data: basic app logs needed for reliability and debugging.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- Provide core app features (finance tracking, budgets, goals, history).
- Sync your data securely across sessions and devices.
- Generate analytics and summaries from your own finance data.
- Send notification reminders that you enable.

## 3. AI Features and Third-Party Processing

When AI features are enabled, selected finance data and prompts may be sent securely to a third-party AI provider to generate insights and responses.

- AI outputs may be inaccurate and should be reviewed carefully.
- PerFin is for informational purposes and does not provide professional legal, tax, or investment advice.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data only with service providers required to operate PerFin, such as:

- Authentication and database infrastructure.
- AI inference providers (only when AI features are used).
- Push/local notification infrastructure.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your account and app data while your account is active.

If you request account deletion, we delete your account data and attempt to permanently remove associated records from active systems, subject to legal requirements and backup retention windows.

## 6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data in transit and at rest. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## 7. Your Choices

- You can disable AI features in Privacy Settings.
- You can manage notification preferences in-app.
- You can request account deletion from Profile > Delete Account.

## 8. Children

PerFin is not intended for children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy over time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date.

## 10. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact: support@perfin.app
